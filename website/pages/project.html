Introduction

This page is dedicated to documenting my final project of EP1000. I would be going through the ideation and design process of how I build my project. For this project, I will be building a clock that uses light to tell the time. I built this because it seems simple enough to make, yet it challenges the concept of what a clock is. It is something I would have fun making and would use at home too! Feel free to follow along to also build your own light clock. Downloads for the files will be included bellow.

Material

Here will be a list of materials needed for the project:

      LED Bulbs x12 (White)
      Arduino Uno x1
      Some pieces of acrylic
      3D printing filament
